Character Sketches

Dr. Sadao Hoki

A skilled Japanese surgeon educated in America, torn between his duty to save lives and national loyalty, ultimately helping an enemy soldier escape.

Hana

Sadao’s supportive wife, compassionate yet conflicted, assists in caring for the prisoner despite societal pressure.

Tom

A young American POW, wounded and defiant, grateful to Sadao for saving his life, escaping to freedom.

The General

An influential patient of Sadao, self-absorbed and pragmatic, plans to eliminate Tom but forgets due to his own health crisis.
